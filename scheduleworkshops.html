Science Workshops - Schedule

Day 1- Saturday, 14th October 2017

  • Morning Session

    •
      10:00 Inaugural Function
      10:30 Introduction to Mentors, Mentors present their hack ideas
      11:00 The Science Show (Aero Show flying RC aircraft)
      12:30 The Science of flight (Demo session with gliders)
      01:00 Lunch (provided)
  • Afternoon Session

    •
      02:00 Hands-on Workshop-I begins
      04:00 High tea
      04:30 students visit exhibit area in groups lead by volunteers
      05:00 The Science Show (Demo Session Newtonian Liquid and Smoke Rings etc...)
      05:30 Parents take the kids home

    Day 2- Sunday, 15th October 2017

  • Morning Session

    •
      10:00 Hands-on workshop-II begins
      01:00 Lunch
  • Afternoon Session

    •
      02:00 SHD Group Photo
      02:15 The Science Show (Demo Session III) In open air..Flying machines, helium balloons etc
      03:15 Students interact with hackers. People/students see the hacks and vote (Parents of registered students are invited at 3.00pm)
      04:00 Snack time... People visit the exhibit area.
      04:30 Valedictary function. Winning teams announced & given SHD India medals
      05:30 Parents take the kids home
