Workshop

Python and Microcontroller Workshops

There will be 2 Python based workshops at SHD India 2019. To attend please book a single ticket and Register here.

Suitable For

Beginner coders, Pre-university Science, Bsc and Engineering students and Physics and Electronics Teachers OR anyone with curios mind and passion to learn new things.

Workshop I - Python 101

Python Programming for Beginners - Hands-on workshop

Come learn the basics of Python, one of the best coding languages for beginner coders. This workshop is perfect for those who have little to no programming experience, or those who just want to learn Python!

Suitable For

Beginner coders, Pre-university Science, Bsc and Engineering students and Teachers

Duration

2 hours 30 minutes (Resource persons will be available for interactions and help for two days)

Workshop II - KuttyPy - Connect to the outside world using Python

Hands-on workshop - Microcontroller training for beginners

Computer programmers and hobbyists at times come across situations where they need to switch a light bulb, rotate a motor or measure a temperature under software control. Generally these things are done by connecting some extra hardware to the computer and coding it in C language. KuttyPy is a USB interfaced board that gives you an option to do those things in pure Python.

Each participant of this workshop will be given KuttyPy Kit to take home

Suitable For

This Hands on workshop is mainly targated at Bsc and Engineering students and also Physics and Electronics Teachers.

Duration

2 hours 30 minutes (Resource persons will be available for interactions and help for two days)


Participants will also get a chance to try some hands on experiments with ExpEYES -python powered open source pocket science lab. It is a hardware & software framework for developing science experiments, demonstrations and projects without getting in to the details of electronics or computer programming. It converts your PC into a science laboratory.
